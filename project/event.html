Classic Motor Show

The Classic Motor Show is one of the largest classic car shows and is held every year at the NEC in Birmingham.
Go along for over 2000 classic cars, 300 classic motorbikes and hundreds of trade stands.
The UK’s biggest car clubs attend too, so it’s a great opportunity to meet like-minded petrolheads.
Hosts from Wheeler Dealers will take over the live stage while restoration demos take place in the theatre. There’s also a two day auction if you’re looking to buy.

8 - 10 November 2018.

GOODWOOD FESTIVAL OF SPEED

The Festival of Speed calls itself the largest and most famous of all the car shows in the calendar.
Held over a long summer weekend at Goodwood Estate in Chichester, the Festival is an extravaganza to celebrate motorsport, from Edwardian classic cars right through to cutting edge supercars and F1 models.
Enjoy the famous Hill Climb and supercar run for your dose of speed, and check out the real life replicas of Wacky Races cars.
Plus mingle with a host of celebrity guests.

5 - 8 July 2019.

Frankfurt Motor Show

The autumn event sees many carmakers showing their new designs and models to the world.
The international motor show is held every two years, alternating with the Paris Motor Show.
See highlights from the incredible German automotive industry and explore the latest concept cars and technological ideas.

14 - 22 September 2019.
